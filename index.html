Payment

We are the first to offer semi-decentralised business model within sharing economy. In this way, we plan to offer two ways of payment: Visa/MasterCard with a serivce fee and proprietary crypto token without a fee.

We plan to issue our crypto token as an alternative way of payment. The cost of the token will be stable until the service gets popular. The token will start being traded after 1 million of deals.
